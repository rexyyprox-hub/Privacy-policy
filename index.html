Welcome to Quizapp. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. By using Quizapp, you agree to the terms described here.

✅ Short version: Quizapp is an offline quiz application. We do not collect personal data, we do not send your data to any server, and we do not share anything with third parties.

1 Information We Collect

Quizapp is designed to function entirely offline. We collect minimal to no personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use analytics tools or tracking SDKs.
  • Quiz progress and scores may be stored locally on your device only.

2 How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to use. Any data stored locally (such as quiz scores or progress) is used solely to:

  • Display your quiz history and results within the app.
  • Improve your experience by remembering where you left off.

This data never leaves your device.

3 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties. There are no advertisements, no advertising networks, and no third-party SDKs embedded in the app that collect data.

4 Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage mechanisms. We implement reasonable security measures within the app. Since no data is transmitted to external servers, the risk of a data breach from our end is eliminated.

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

5 Offline Use

Quizapp is built for offline viewers. The app does not require an internet connection to function. We do not make any network requests during normal app usage.

6 Children's Privacy

Quizapp does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any data from any user, it is safe for all age groups. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us.

7 Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on any server, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our end. You can remove all locally stored data by:

  • Going to Settings → Apps → Quizapp → Clear Data on your Android device.
  • Uninstalling the application.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
